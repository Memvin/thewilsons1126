FAQ

Who is invited?
Please note that the invitation is only extended to those named on the invitation. This will be an adults-only celebration (no children of any age).
When do I need to RSVP by?
Please RSVP by Sunday, 27th September 2026. The RSVP form includes dietary requirements/allergies, song requests and a section for you to ask questions, give us advice or (if you wish) share a memory with us.
Where is the wedding?
Sittella Winery, 100 Barrett Street, Herne Hill WA 6056
How do I get there?
Driving is the easiest option. Free parking is available at the venue. There is no public transport to Sittella. We will do our best to assist with shared rides if you note it on your RSVP. While Uber and DiDi are available, taxis might be more reliable later in the evening.
What is the dress code?
Smart casual. Please avoid black, white, or red. No ties or shorts, thank you.
What is the weather like?
November can be anything from cool and rainy to hot and sunny. Ceremony and reception areas are covered, rain or shine. Please use sunscreen (we’ll have some available), stay hydrated, and consider bringing a light jacket for the evening.
Is it an unplugged ceremony?
Yes — we kindly ask that phones and cameras be put away during the ceremony. After the ceremony, please feel free to take photos and use #TheWilsons1126, but please hold off posting the newlyweds until after the ceremony.
Can we post on social media?
Yes — we’d love that. Please wait until after the ceremony, and feel free to use our hashtag #TheWilsons1126. (And please avoid posting the newlyweds before the ceremony finishes.)
What’s the schedule?
Please arrive by 3:30pm for a 4:00pm ceremony start. Cocktail hour, dinner and dancing will follow. We’ll conclude the night at 11:30pm.
Gifts / wishing well?
Your presence is truly all we ask for. If you’d like to include a card, a wishing well will be available on the day.
